Frequently Asked Questions

What is veterinary telemedicine?

Veterinary telemedicine is the use of technology to provide veterinary care remotely. It allows pet and livestock owners to access expert care for their furry companions from the comfort of their own home.
What services does GetVet offer?

GetVet offers a range of services, including 24/7 chat and video call consultations, home consultations, a diagnostic lab directory, and a vaccination tracker. We also have a blog where we provide educational articles.
Who are the veterinarians that provide the consultations?

Our team of veterinarians are experienced and qualified to provide expert advice and guidance for your pet's or livestock's health concerns. They are available for you 24/7.

Can I get a prescription for medication through GetVet?

Yes, our veterinarians can provide prescriptions for medication through a telemedicine consultation. However, it's important to note that some medications may require an inperson examination before a prescription can be written.
What if I need to see a veterinarian in person?

If our veterinarians determine that an in-person examination is necessary, they will refer you to opt for our home consultation services to provide you with the best possible care for your furry companions or visit a local veterinarian or veterinary clinic service.

How much does it cost to use GetVet’s services?and how can i pay

The cost of our services varies depending on the service you choose. You can find more information about pricing on our website. We accept various payments methods, including credit card, Paystack, and bank transfer.
